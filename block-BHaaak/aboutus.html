About Wedme_good

Wedme_good is a wedding portal and directory. Our mission is to provide online platform or market place that provides valuable wedding-related information for the modern prospective couple. The services offered by us include the WedMeGood websites located at wedmegood.com or any sub domain of wedmegood.com (the "WedMeGood Website"), and any other feature, content or applications offered from time to time by WedMeGood in connection with the WedMeGood Website whether accessed directly or through our application for mobile devices (collectively, the "WedMeGood Services").
